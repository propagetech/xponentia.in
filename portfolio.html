Portfolio

Xponentia focuses on investing in the Indian mid-market corporate sector, providing capital and capabilities to help companies scale and grow.

Across Xponentia Opportunities Fund-1 and Xponentia Opportunities Fund-2, the firm now manages ~$200 million of investor capital.

Investment Focus

We invest in mid-market companies that:

  • Have dynamic and enterprising entrepreneurs building world class businesses
  • Are at a stage where they need capital to scale
  • Would benefit from corporatization and strengthening of functional capabilities
  • Have potential for long-term sustainable growth

For more information about our portfolio companies, please contact us.
