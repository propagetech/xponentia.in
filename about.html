About Us

Xponentia was set up to address the paucity of capital in the Indian mid-market corporate sector. Inspite of having dynamic and enterprising entrepreneurs who are building world class businesses, this segment of the Indian market has traditionally been faced with a dearth of capital providers – both debt and equity.

Moreover, at this stage of their life-cycle, in addition to capital, companies need to "corporatize" their business in order to scale up - they need to strengthen functional capabilities in finance, marketing, IT and HR as well as improve corporate governance practices, important elements for long-term sustainable growth. Xponentia aims to be a value added partner who can catalyse sustainable growth by providing capabilities & capital to our portfolio companies.

Leveraging decades of investing experience, Xponentia has developed a unique Value Creation Strategy that provides a compelling value proposition to our portfolio companies with demonstrated results.

With a successful track record of investing over $1billion of capital, Xponentia's Founders have a long and successful history in the Indian market for over three decades, and previously led the investment teams of The Carlyle Group and Citigroup Venture Capital International (CVCI) & HSBC Private Equity.

Xponentia has assembled a Team of Investment Professionals with extensive industry experience at marquee firms such as Oman-India Joint Investment Fund, Rabo Private Equity, Edelweiss, SAIF Partners, Deloitte and Motilal Oswal, and retained the services of Operating Advisors/Management Consultants - seasoned functional specialists in Strategy, Finance, Marketing, HR and IT - who work actively with our entrepreneurs/management teams and assist them into transforming their businesses into world class enterprises.

Across Xponentia Opportunities Fund-1 and Xponentia Opportunities Fund-2, the firm now manages ~$200 million of investor capital.
