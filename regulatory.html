Scam Alert

We have become aware that certain unknown individuals have created WhatsApp groups using the name of Xponentia Group and our employees to share investment advice. We want to clarify that neither Xponentia Group, its affiliates, nor any of our employees are associated with these groups. There may be other similar illegitimate platforms misusing our name.

Xponentia Group is a private equity firm and a regulated entity. We do not provide advice on public equities, nor do we promote or solicit investments through WhatsApp, Telegram, websites, apps, or any other electronic platforms. Any reliance on or payments made through such platforms is at your own risk, and Xponentia Group will not be responsible.

If someone misrepresents themselves as being affiliated with Xponentia Group, you may consider filing a police complaint and informing the cyber-crime cell. We have already initiated steps to report these fraudulent activities to the authorities and relevant agencies, and we are informing individuals who have reached out to us regarding such instances.

Thank you for your understanding and attention.
